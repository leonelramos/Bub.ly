Configuration

Color accuracy is a threshold dictating how unique two colors must be in order to be considered different colors. For example if the image has three different shades of red, all with similar r,g,b values, a lower accuracy would treat them as the same color while a higher accuracy would recognize them as seperate colors.

The render limit is a limit to the amount of bubbles Bub.ly will generate. This does not guarentee that Bub.ly will generate as many bubbles as this limit. The amount of bubbles gerated is dependant on the color accuracy and how many colors the image has. Render limit simply sets a cap on the number of bubles created.
